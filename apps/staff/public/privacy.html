Privacy Policy

Celsius Staff app · operated by Celsius Coffee Sdn Bhd · Last updated 26 May 2026

The Celsius Staff app (the "App") is an internal workforce tool for employees of Celsius Coffee Sdn Bhd ("we", "us", "Celsius"). The App is not available to the public and access is provisioned only for current employees. This policy explains what personal data the App collects, why we collect it, and how we handle it.

1. Who can use the App

The App is restricted to active Celsius Coffee staff. You can only sign in with a PIN issued to you by Celsius. There is no public sign-up. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 16.

2. Data we collect

2.1 Account & HR data

  • Your full name, staff number, role, assigned outlet(s) and permissions — pulled from your Celsius employee record.
  • IC number, address, emergency contact, bank details and dependants — only the fields you choose to fill in for payroll, EPF, SOCSO and EIS submissions.

2.2 Attendance

  • Clock-in / clock-out timestamps, the GPS coordinates recorded at each clock event, and a selfie taken at clock-in and clock-out for identity verification.
  • When you opt in to background location, the App checks whether you are inside or outside your outlet's geofence at regular intervals. We record only whether you crossed a boundary — not your continuous location.

2.3 Operational data you create

  • Checklist completions, audit ratings + photos, stock counts, wastage entries, transfers, receivings, expense claims, purchase orders and invoices that you submit through the App — all tied to your user ID for accountability.

2.4 Device data

  • A push notification token (issued by Apple or Google) so we can deliver shift reminders and approval alerts.
  • Basic crash and performance telemetry (Sentry) to help us fix bugs. This does not include the contents of your screens.

3. Permissions we ask for

  • Location (foreground & optional background): to detect which outlet you are at for attendance.
  • Camera: to take attendance selfies, audit photos and receipt photos for expense claims.
  • Photo library: to attach existing photos (e.g. invoices) to claims.
  • Face ID / Touch ID: optional second factor for clock-in. The biometric check happens entirely on your device; Apple/Google never share the biometric data with us and we never see it.
  • Notifications: for shift reminders and approval requests.

4. How we use the data

  • To roster shifts, calculate hours worked and run payroll.
  • To verify that staff are physically present at the outlet at clock-in.
  • To deliver SOPs, audits and operational tasks to the right person at the right time.
  • To process expense claims and supplier invoices.
  • To improve the App — bug reports, performance, crash diagnostics.

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for marketing.

5. Where the data is stored

Your data is stored in our own Postgres database (hosted on Supabase, Singapore region) and in object storage for photos (Supabase Storage and Cloudinary). Backups are encrypted at rest.

6. Who can see your data

  • You can see your own data anytime in the App (Profile, Personal info, Payslips, etc.).
  • Your managers and outlet leads can see the operational data (attendance, checklists, audits, claims) tied to outlets they manage.
  • Celsius HR and Finance can see the data needed for payroll, EPF/SOCSO/EIS submissions and statutory reporting.
  • We share data with regulators (LHDN, KWSP, PERKESO) only where required by Malaysian law.
  • Third-party processors we use: Supabase (database + storage), Cloudinary (image hosting), Sentry (crash reporting), Expo (push delivery and OTA updates), Apple (APNs) and Google (FCM). Each is bound by their own data processing terms.

7. How long we keep data

  • Attendance and payroll data: 7 years after the end of employment, as required by Malaysian tax and labour law.
  • Operational data (checklists, audits, wastage, claims, invoices): 7 years for audit purposes.
  • Crash telemetry: 90 days, then auto-deleted.
  • Push tokens: deleted within 30 days of you signing out.

8. Your rights

Under the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act 2010 you can ask Celsius to:

  • see a copy of the data we hold about you;
  • correct anything that's wrong;
  • stop processing your data for non-statutory purposes (you can always sign out and stop using the App);
  • delete your data when your employment ends, subject to the retention periods above.

Submit any request to hr@celsiuscoffee.com. We will respond within 21 days.

9. Security

All traffic between the App and our servers is over HTTPS. Your session is authenticated with a signed JWT and revoked immediately when you sign out, change your PIN or are removed from Celsius. Photos are uploaded directly to signed-URL endpoints; we never bounce them through an intermediate server.

10. Children

The App is not intended for and is not offered to children under 16. If you believe a minor has been given access to the App, contact us and we will revoke it.

11. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, the in-app login screen will show a notice the next time you sign in. The updated date is shown at the top of this page.
