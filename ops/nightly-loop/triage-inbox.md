# Triage inbox — for the human

Anything the nightly loop **cannot or should not** handle lands here for you:
findings that are too large, ambiguous, architecturally significant, or that
touch money / inventory / customer-facing behaviour where a wrong "plausible"
fix is dangerous. The loop is designed to punt freely — an honest punt is a
success, not a failure.

Read this with your morning coffee. Clear items by either doing them yourself,
or reshaping them into something small enough to hand back to the loop.
